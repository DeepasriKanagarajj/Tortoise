>> the story behind the signal
>> CHAPTER_01 // TALLINN

my name is deva.

i lived in tallinn for a few years. quiet forests. long winters. the sound of snow under your feet and nothing else for miles.

it was beautiful. and it was isolating in ways i didn't have the words for yet.

i was living inside something i didn't have a name for. an abusive relationship i thought was normal. i thought love was supposed to hurt. i thought staying was strength. i thought if i just tried harder—

i didn't see it. not clearly. not until much later.

🍒    🐢    🍒
>> CHAPTER_02 // FLORENCE

then came a trip to florence. i was trying to breathe. trying to escape the weight i'd been carrying.

i met a stranger. a beautiful one. we talked for a few hours. they said something i have never forgotten.

"love isn't supposed to make you small.
it's supposed to make you feel like you can breathe."

i never saw them again. but that conversation cracked something open.

i started to understand that feeling safe, feeling seen, feeling like you don't have to pretend — that's not too much to ask for. that's the minimum.

🍒    🐢    🍒
>> CHAPTER_03 // WHY TORTOISE EXISTS

because no one deserves to carry something heavy with nowhere to put it down.

because your family might not understand. your friends are busy. and sometimes saying "i'm not okay" out loud feels harder than disappearing.

BUT HUMANS EXIST WHO WILL MAKE YOU FEEL LIKE YOU MATTER.

sometimes they're strangers on a train. sometimes they're someone you'll never see again. sometimes they're someone who picks up because they chose to show up today.

they exist. you exist. you matter.

tortoise is for the moments when you need to hear that. from a human. not an algorithm. not a script.

just: "i hear you. i'm here. you're not alone."

i'm building this slowly. like a tortoise. because the things that last are built with time — not noise.

made with more than code.
🐢 deva bangkok, thailand
