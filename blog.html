>> SEP 22, 2025
why slow matters

the world moves fast. we don't have to.

when you feel unheard, when you feel lonely, when you feel invisible — the last thing you need is something fast. fast feels cold. fast feels like you're just another notification.

slow feels different. slow feels like someone is actually paying attention. like they're not in a hurry to leave.

that's why this is tortoise. because we're building something that stays. something that doesn't rush you. something that waits until you're ready.

if you're reading this, thank you for being slow with me.

🐢 slowly, deva

>> MAY 5, 2024
what i'm building and why

someone asked me today: "why an app for loneliness? isn't that what friends are for?"

yes. and no.

friends are wonderful. but friends aren't always there at 3am. friends don't always understand the kind of quiet that feels like drowning. and sometimes, telling a friend "i'm not okay" feels harder than sitting alone.

tortoise is for those moments. not to replace friends. but to be there when friends can't be.

a human who just listens. no advice. no fixing. just "i hear you. i'm here."

that's all. that's everything.

🐢 slowly, deva

>> FEB 20, 2024
the cherry story

why a cherry?

people ask. they see the cherry next to the tortoise and wonder.

cherries are small. they're sweet. they don't try to be a whole meal. they're just a moment.

when you're lonely, you don't need someone to fix everything. sometimes you just need a small sweetness. someone to sit with you. someone to say "i see you."

the cherry is that small sweetness.

🍒 sweetly, deva
